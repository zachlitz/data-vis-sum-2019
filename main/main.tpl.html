What's The Weather Really Like In Phoenix, Arizona, USA?

When most people think of Phoenix, Arizona, the words "sun", "hot", "dry", and "desert" usually come to mind.

Called the "Valley of the Sun", Phoenix is known for it's dry desert climate and its consistently extreme summer time temperatures. But how hot is it really? Is it as dry as they say? Should it really be called the Valley of the Sun?

We're going to explore climate data from the NOAA in order to get a better idea of what Phoenix weather is really like and to see if Phoenix's reputation for being a very hot metro is accurate, or just hyperbole.
