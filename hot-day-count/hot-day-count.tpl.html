Hot Days Per Month In Phoenix Last Year (2018)

We saw from the last chart that there were a considerable amount of days where the daily high temperature was 100°F (38°C) or higher.

In most places across the US, we would consider the "hot" months to be those which fall in the summer months (from roughly June to September). But we've already seen that in Phoenix, 100°F temperatures exist for six months of the year. Let's explore this more.

Use the slider to control the cutoff temperature. The chart below will update to show the percentage of days per month and per year where the high temperature was equal or above the cutoff temperature.
